I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at Southern Methodist University, specializing in applied economics at the intersection of culture, media, and gender.

I will join the University of Zurich as a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Management and Economics of Digital Transformation group in August 2026.

A key aim of my research is to use generative AI to measure values from cultural markets (e.g., TV series, songs, movies, and books) at a massive scale. In addition, I am interested in examining how gender norms shape labor market behaviors for both men and women, and their broader aggregate economic consequences.
