Telenovela Data (Work in Progress)

This dataset compiles information on a wide range of telenovelas, including plot summaries, episode lengths, and broadcast details for LATAM covering 1960-2024. In addition to basic show metadata, it features coded indicators of cultural values such as inequality, female empowerment, immigration, and environmental protection as reflected in the storylines. The dataset enables analysis of how these themes are represented and evolve across different countries in Latin America through time.
